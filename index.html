Privacy Policy — Youth. App

Last updated: March 27, 2026

1. Introduction

Youth. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Youth. mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you register, we collect your email address, first and last name, and an optional profile photo.

Usage Data: We collect songs you mark as favorites, groups you create or join, and songs shared within groups.

Feedback: If you submit feedback, we collect your message, account email, and device platform.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to create and manage your account, display your name to group members, enable real-time song sharing, respond to feedback, and improve the app.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the United States. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Groups & Sharing

When you join a group, your display name and profile photo are visible to other members of that group. Song sharing only occurs within groups you have explicitly joined.

6. Children's Privacy

Youth. is intended for users of all ages within a church youth group setting. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. Contact us at youthmovement.app@gmail.com if you have concerns.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion by contacting youthmovement.app@gmail.com.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at youthmovement.app@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

9. Third Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and data storage (Google Privacy Policy).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected in the updated date at the top.

11. Contact Us

Questions? Email us at youthmovement.app@gmail.com
